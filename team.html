Welcome to Damascus Cosmetic and Laser Dental Center, the offices of Dr. Steven McKane. In order to better serve you we are excited to announce our state-of-the-art office in Mount Airy, located in the 1311 Main Street Plaza.

Damascus Dental Center is proud to offer the most advanced techniques in modern dentistry. Specializing in laser dentistry our premium services include root canals, crowns, veneers, dentures and much more. We are a mercury-free dental facility, protecting the environment for our children and future generations to come.

In keeping with our tradition of groundbreaking technology we are proud to be one of the first offices in the area to offer Laser Periodontal Therapy. Did you know that Laser Dentistry can lessen the amount of time it takes to fill a cavity and reduce the recovery time after periodontal treatment? Laser Dentistry has had a significant impact on modern dentistry. Improved comfort, incredible results and stress free visits are just a few of the benefits enjoyed by patients at Damascus Cosmetic & Laser Dental Center.

We pride ourselves on providing uncompromisingly high standard of dental services. We believe that dentistry should not involve anxiety or pain.

At Damascus Dental Center our primary concern is patient care. As we continue to seek out new and innovative technology we are careful never to lose sight of the high value we place on customer service. We are constantly examining and refining every detail of our patient's experience. This added attention to detail, paired with our outstanding team member, is what makes us stand out from the rest.

Meet the Team


Dr. Steven McKane, DDS
Dr. McKane received his Bachelor of Science Degree from Mount St. Mary's University with a BS in Biology. He earned his DDS from Baltimore College of Dental Surgery at the University of Maryland. He is an active member in the Academy of Laser Dentistry. He also participates in numerous hours of continuing education each year. He resides in Frederick where he lives with his wife and son.

Dr. Avadis Abrahamian, DDS
Dr. Avadis Abrahamian has been the chairman of the Department if Orthodontics at Howard University for the past seven years. He has tought Orthodontics for over 22 years and is a member of the dental team at Howard University. He received his D.D.S from the National University of Iran (1972) and went on to receive his masters in orthodontics at Georgetown University in 1978.
